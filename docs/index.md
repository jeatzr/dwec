4. **[Manipulating the DOM](04_DOM/README.md)**
5. **[Event Management and Form Validation](05_events_forms/README.md)**
6. **[AJAX](06_AJAX/README.md)**
7. **[Client-Side Storage](07_local_storage/README.md)**
